On The Surface

Redesigned. Redefined. How boba should be: to your liking. Bobaddict aggregates rating and number of ratings of boba tea that these boba shops sell. Use these ratings to your advantage, as your peers have been sippin' on and spillin' it.

How It Works

Give us a value between 0 and 100% because 1 to 10 is lame. How’d you like it? Think real hard about it, because your rating matters. Just a simple percent is all we’re asking for. If you’re confused, the percent is not the sugar level, so don’t sugarcoat it.
